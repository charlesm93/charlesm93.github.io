Reading list
---

### Week 2

* <span style="color:Salmon">For how many iterations should we run MCMC?</span> Margossian and Gelman. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.02726)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Rank-normalization, folding, and localization: an improved R for assessing convergence of MCMC</span> Vehtari et al. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.08008)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Probabilistic Inference using Markov chain Monte Carlo methods</span> Neal. Sections 3–4. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/physics/0609001)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">General State Space Markov chains and MCMC algorithms</span> Roberts and Rosenthal. Sections 1–3. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0404033)]

### Week 3

* <span style="color:Salmon">Practical Bayesian Model Evaluation using Leave-One-Out Cross-Validation and WAIC</span> Vehtari, Gelman, and Gabry. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1507.04544)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">A Conceptual Introduction to Hamiltonian Monte Carlo</span> Betancourt. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1701.02434)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">MCMC using Hamiltonian dynamics</span> Neal. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1206.1901)]

### Week 4

* <span style="color:Salmon">Adaptively Setting Path Lengths in Hamiltonian Monte Carlo</span> Hoffman and Gelman. [[pdf](https://jmlr.org/papers/volume15/hoffman14a/hoffman14a.pdf)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">An Adaptive MCMC Scheme for Setting Trajectory Lengths in Hamiltonian Monte Carlo</span> Hoffman, Radul and Sountsov. [[pdf](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v130/hoffman21a.html)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Nested R: Assessing the Convergence of MCMC when running many short chains</span> Margossian et al. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.13017)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Estimating Convergence of Markov chain Monte Carlo with L-lag Coupling</span> Biswas, Jacob and Vanetti. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.09971)]


### Week 5

* <span style="color:Salmon">Variational Inference: A Review for Statisticians</span> Blei, Kucukelbir and McAuliffe. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1601.00670)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Yes but did it work? Evaluating Variational Inference</span> Yao et al. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.02538)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Validated variational inference via practical posterior error bounds</span> Huggins et al. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.04102)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Pathfinder: Parallel quasi-Newton variational inference</span> Zhang et al. [[pdf](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.03782)]

### Week 6

* <span style="color:Salmon">Bayesian Workflow</span>. Gelman, Vehtari, McElreath et al. Any one chapter in Part IV.
* <span style="color:Salmon">Philosophy and the Practice of Bayesian Analysis</span> Gelman and Shalizi. [[pdf](https://sites.stat.columbia.edu/gelman/research/published/philosophy.pdf)]
* <span style="color:Salmon">Towards a Principled Bayesian Workflow</span> Betancourt [[link](https://betanalpha.github.io/assets/case_studies/principled_bayesian_workflow.html)]
